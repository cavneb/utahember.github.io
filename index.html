Who are you?

The Utah Ember Users Group ("UtahEmber") is a mostly informal collection of individuals interested in the Ember javascript framework.

When do you meet?

Salt Lake County

4th Thursday of the month at 6:30 PM
Instructure offices

Schedule of meetups including notes for upcoming meetings at meetup.com

How do I join?

Utah Ember membership is free and with no obligations. Just show up to the meetings periodically, and you're welcome to call yourself a member of UtahEmber User Group.

Want to discuss Ember.js?

IRC: freenode #utahember
